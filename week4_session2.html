Week 4 – Upper Body Balance

Mix pressing, rowing and shoulder‑friendly overhead work.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10 reps. Maintain a neutral grip for shoulder comfort.
  • Bent‑Over Row: 3 sets of 10 reps. Keep torso hinged at 45 degrees and row elbows towards hips.
  • Single‑Arm Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps per arm using a light dumbbell. Brace your core and avoid arching your back.
  • Rotator Cuff Series (Y‑T‑W): 3 sets of 8 of each movement using light weights or bands. Form a “Y,” “T,” and “W” with your arms while squeezing shoulder blades.
  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 30‑second holds per side.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
