Sanbon Kumite No. 1 — Jōdan, Chūdan, Mae Geri

HEAD–UP → BODY–OUT → KICK–DOWN → FINISH MIDDLE

Say it as you move: “Head-Up, Body-Out, Kick-Down — Finish Middle.”

Attacker calls: “Jōdan, Chūdan, Mae Geri!”
Attack 1
Jōdan punch (oi-zuki)
Cue: aim to the head.
Defence 1
Age-uke (rising block). Think: UP to cover the head.
Attack 2
Chūdan punch (oi-zuki)
Cue: aim to the body.
Defence 2
Soto-ude-uke (outside middle block). Think: push OUT from centre.
Attack 3
Mae-geri (front kick)
Cue: incoming kick.
Defence 3
Gedan-barai (downward sweep). Think: knock it DOWN.
Finish
Immediate counter: Gyaku-zuki (chūdan). Think: FINISH MIDDLE.

Rhythm & Form (keep it simple)

  • Same hip height throughout; step cleanly, no bobbing.
  • Time breath with each move: inhale on prep, exhale on block/counter.
  • On blocks, rotate wrist + hips together at impact.
  • After counter, reset to kamae with awareness.
