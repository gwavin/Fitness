Conventions & Notes on Sidedness
• This sheet uses the common pattern where the attacker steps R-L-R (toggle above). Mirroring yields L-R-L.
• The defender always retreats the same side as the attack, so the front arm performs the block each time.
• For kicks (step 3), retreat slightly to the outside of the kicking line for clearance and stability.
• Degrees (10°, 15°, 30°, 90°) are cues, not absolutes—adjust for your body and dojo conventions.
Practice notes: Keep head level, breathe naturally, maintain 間合いma-aicombative distance, and give a crisp 気合kiaispirit shout on the third attack and the finish.
