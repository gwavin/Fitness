Week 6 – Upper Body & Overhead Work

Introduce controlled overhead pressing and rowing while preserving shoulder health.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Seated/Standing Dumbbell Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps with light dumbbells. Keep ribs down and avoid arching your back.
  • Chest‑Supported Row: 3 sets of 10 reps lying prone on an incline bench; row dumbbells towards your hips.
  • Push‑Up or Dumbbell Chest Press: 3 sets of 10 reps. Choose the variation that feels comfortable.
  • Band Pull‑Apart & External Rotation: 3 sets of 15 reps each. Strengthen the rear delts and rotator cuff.
  • Side Plank with Reach Through: 3 sets of 10 reps per side. From side plank, rotate your top arm underneath your body and extend upwards.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
