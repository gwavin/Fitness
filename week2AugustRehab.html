Anytime Micro-Pack

Always-green moves for downtime. Pain ≤3/10; stop for sharp/tingly.

1) Scap Set + Long Exhale — 6–10 breaths
  • Sit tall. Gentle shrug → roll back → slide blades down into back pockets.
  • Quiet inhale; 6–8 s exhale; feel blades settle.
2) Wall/Seated “Plus” — 2×8–10 easy
  • Hands on wall (or elbows on thighs). Ribs down. Gently protract → return; no shrug.
3) Couch Pendulums — 20–30 s patterns
  • Support the good hand; other arm hangs. Tiny circles from body sway; then forward/back.
4) Wrist Extensor Calm Set — 1×20–30 s
  • Forearm supported, palm down. Press on knuckles with the other hand and match (3–4/10).
5) Seated T-Spine Rotations — 5–6/side
  • Hands across chest; exhale as you rotate ribs; stop before any shoulder pinch.
6) Short-Foot — 3×10–15 s/side
  • Lift arch without curling toes; breathe.
7) Chin-Tuck + Turn — 5 slow turns/side
  • Light double-chin; slow comfortable turns; relax jaw/shoulders.
Week 2 Defaults
  • Goblet Squat, RDL: 2×8 @ RPE ≤6 (was 2×6–8). Add reps before load.
  • Elbow isometrics: 20–30s ×3–4 @ 4–5/10 (optional +1 hold vs W1).
  • Run: 6× (45s jog / 75s walk) with 5′ warm-up/cool-down.
  • Neutral grips; straps OK on hinge if elbow talks. Pain ≤3/10 during & next day.

Monday — Weights + Elbow Care (~35 min)

Pendulum Swings — 30s each direction
  • Lean forward, support with the other hand; working arm hangs relaxed.
  • Tiny circles from body sway; breathe; add forward/back if easy.
Open Books (T-spine) — 6/side
  • Side-lying; hips/knees 90°. Rotate top arm open while knees stay stacked; exhale into range.
Doorway Pec Stretch (optional; safe angle) — 20–30s ×1
  • Elbow below shoulder (45–60°); set scapula down/back; rotate chest through the doorway; keep it 2–3/10.
Goblet Squat — 2×8 @ RPE ≤6 • 3-1-1 tempo +1 rep/set vs W1
  • KB/DB at chest; sit between hips; 3s down • 1s pause • drive up; heels down.
Romanian Deadlift (DB/KB) — 2×8 very light–light +1 rep/set vs W1
  • Soft knees; hinge hips back; lats packed; weights skim thighs; straps OK if elbow sensitive.
Wrist Extensor Isometric — 20–30s ×3–4 @ 4–5/10
  • Forearm supported, palm down. Gently press hand into resistance; match without moving.
Triceps Isometric (wall) — 15–20s ×3
  • Palm to wall; elbow ~60–90°; press as if straightening; shoulder stays down/back.
Scapular Setting — 5s ×10
  • S hrug → roll back → set blades down into back pockets; wide collarbones; easy neck.
Band Internal Rotation — 2×12–15 (very light, 3s return)
  • Elbow glued to side at 90°; pull forearm toward belly; slow 3s return.
Wall Angels — 2×8
  • Back/head/butt to wall; ribs down; slide to clean range; no winging.
Push-Up Plus (wall) — 1×12
  • Small push-up; at top, protract blades (the “plus”); no shrug/rib flare.

Tuesday — Barefoot Walk–Jog + Foot/Calf Prep (~25–30 min)

Short-Foot Drill — 3×20s/side

Double-Leg Calf Raise — 3×12 slow
  • 2–3s up/down; rise over big/second toe.
Tibialis Raise — 2×15

Run – Session A — 5′ walk → 6× (45s jog / 75s walk) → 5′ walk

  • Next-morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min → repeat this volume.

Wall Angels — 2×8
