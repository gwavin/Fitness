Week 7 – Mobility & Conditioning

Use dynamic movements and kettlebell complexes to improve mobility and endurance.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Kettlebell Swing: 3 sets of 15 reps. Focus on explosive hip drive.
  • Overhead Lunge: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg holding dumbbells overhead.
  • Kettlebell Clean & Press: 3 sets of 8 reps per arm. Maintain tight core and straight wrist.
  • Goblet Squat with Pause: 3 sets of 10 reps. Hold the bottom position for 2 seconds.
  • Russian Twist: 3 sets of 20 reps. Keep spine neutral and rotate through the torso.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
