Therapeutic Drug Monitoring (TDM) targets

Once-daily (adult)
Pre-dose < 1 mg/L
Take level 18–24 h after last dose; before 2nd dose
Paediatrics
Trough < 1 mg/L
First trough < 2 mg/L acceptable; then < 1 mg/L
Neonates
Trough < 1 mg/L
Before 2nd dose; hold if ≥ 1 mg/L
Synergy (endo)
Peak 3–5 mg/L
Trough < 1 mg/L; interval by CrCl

Administration & precautions

  • IV infusion: add total dose to 100 mL NaCl 0.9% or Glucose 5% and infuse over ~20 minutes. Avoid rapid bolus.
  • Available strength commonly: 80 mg/2 mL (40 mg/mL). Prototype rounds to nearest mL for volume display.
  • Weight for dose: If Actual Body Weight (ABW) ≤ 120% of Ideal Body Weight (IBW) use ABW; otherwise use Dose-Determining Weight (DDW) = IBW + 0.4 × (ABW − IBW).
  • Creatinine clearance (CrCl): Cockcroft–Gault; defaults to IBW (or ABW if underweight). Creatinine unit auto-converted (µmol/L ↔ mg/dL).
  • Toxicity: Nephrotoxicity & cochlear/vestibular ototoxicity risk ↑ with higher troughs, prolonged courses, renal impairment, dehydration, age, and co-nephrotoxins. Daily renal review advised during therapy.
  • Pregnancy: Use only if benefits outweigh risks; seek specialist advice. Breastfeeding: generally compatible; monitor infant as clinically indicated.
Formulas & caps for transparency

IBW (Devine)

Male: 50 + 2.3×(inches over 60); Female: 45.5 + 2.3×(inches over 60). Height in inches = cm / 2.54.

Cockcroft–Gault

CrCl = ((140 − age) × weight × sexFactor) / (72 × SCrmg/dL). sexFactor = 1 (M) or 0.85 (F). Weight: IBW by default (ABW if underweight).

Adult dosing bands

CrCl > 50 → 5 mg/kg OD (max 480 mg); 30–50 → 4 mg/kg OD; 10–30 → 3 mg/kg OD; 5–10 → 2 mg/kg stat; HD/CAPD/CVVH: consult renal.

Paeds & Neonates

Paeds ≥1 month: 7 mg/kg every 24 h (max 560 mg) with trough-guided interval. Neonates: 5 mg/kg every 36 h if ≤6 days old, otherwise every 24 h; trough <1 mg/L.
