Week 5 — Shoulder + Elbow Rehab with Barefoot Run

Building endurance + shoulder stability
How to use this page
  • Click any exercise to open the full how‑to, cues, mistakes, and regressions/progressions.
  • Pain rule: 0–3/10 is acceptable. Stop for sharp/catching pain or if soreness >24h next day.
  • Week 5 progression: maintain 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 for main lifts with small increases in shoulder exercise complexity. Running volume increases slightly with one additional interval.
Week 5 Defaults
  • Goblet Squat, RDL: 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7. Continue progressive overload: +1 rep on first set if form is clean, or micro‑load 2.5 kg if at 8 reps.
  • Upper add‑ins: Chest‑Supported Row (neutral) 3×10–12; Incline Push‑Up Plus 2–3×10–15 (progress toward floor version).
  • Elbow: continue Eccentric wrist extension 3×8–12 (3s down) + pronation/supination 2–3×15. Add light wrist flexor stretch if needed.
  • Run sessions: 7× (90s jog / 60s walk) between 5′ warm‑up/cool‑down (≈10.5 min jog total). Repeat volume if next‑morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min.
  • Grip neutral where possible; straps allowed on hinge work if elbow talks.

Week 5 – Alternating Days (Weights ↔ Run) + Daily Shoulder Rehab

Maintain shoulder‑safe angles and cues. Progress only when next‑day symptoms are ≤3/10. Use the buttons below to expand/collapse details.

Monday — Weights + Elbow Care (~45–50 min)

Pendulum Swings — 30s each direction
  • Lean forward, hand on a table/chair. Let the affected arm hang completely relaxed.
  • Start tiny circles from body momentum, not the shoulder muscles. Breathe slowly.
  • Clockwise/counter‑clockwise, then gentle forward/back and side/side if comfortable.
Open Books (T‑spine) — 8 reps/side volume increase
  • Side‑lying; hips/knees 90°. Reach top hand up, then open toward the floor behind you while knees stay stacked.
  • Breathe out into the range; no pinching. Focus on thoracic rotation.
Goblet Squat — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 • 3‑1‑1 tempo
  • KB/DB at chest; feet shoulder‑width; brace; sit between hips; chest tall; knees track over mid‑foot.
  • 3s down • 1s pause • drive up over mid‑foot. Heels stay down.
Romanian Deadlift (DB/KB) — 3×6–8 light–moderate
  • Soft knees; hinge hips back; spine neutral; lats packed; weights skim thighs; stop mid‑shin; 2s up. Straps ok if elbow talks.
Chest‑Supported Row (neutral grip) — 3×10–12
  • Bench 30–45°; chest down; palms facing. Depress/retract blades slightly; row elbows 30–45° from body; pause 1s; lower 2–3s. Neck long; no shrug.
  • Cranking with biceps; shoulders hiking; elbows at 90° abduction.
Incline Push‑Up Plus — 2–3×10–15 progression available
  • Hands on bench/step; body straight; push‑up; at top, actively protract shoulder blades (plus). If too easy, lower incline height.
  • Neck long; no rib flare or hip sag.
Eccentric Wrist Extension — 3×8–12 (3s eccentric)
  • Light weight; forearm supported; slowly lower 3s; use opposite hand to assist up if needed. Focus on control.
Pronation/Supination — 2–3×15
  • Light hammer/weight; forearm supported; rotate palm up/down; smooth rhythm.

Tuesday — Run + Calf/Shin Prep + Daily Shoulder Rehab (~45 min)

Short‑Foot Drill — 3×20s/side
  • Lift arch gently; avoid toe curling; breathe normally; maintain for full 20s.
Double‑Leg Calf Raise — 3×15 slow volume increase
  • Full range; 2–3s up/down; rise over big/second toe; pause briefly at top; no bouncing.
Tibialis Raise — 2×15
  • Back to wall; heels down; lift toes toward shins; control the lower; keep knees soft.
Run – Week 5 Session A — 5' walk → 7× (90s jog / 60s walk) → 5' walk volume increase
  • Cadence ~170–180; land under center; quiet feet; posture tall with slight forward lean from ankles.
  • If next‑morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min, repeat Week 4 volume before progressing.
Scapular Setting — 5s ×12 volume increase
  • Gentle shrug → roll back → set blades down into back pockets; neck relaxed; breathe. Think wide collarbones; no pinching between blades.
Band Internal Rotation — 2×12–15 (very light, 3s return)
  • Anchor at elbow height; elbow glued to side at 90°; pull forearm toward belly; pause; slow 3s return.
Wall Angels — 2×10 volume increase
  • Back/head/butt to wall; ribs down; slide up only to clean range; no winging or arching. Smooth breathing.
Push‑Up Plus (wall or incline) — 1×15 progression available
  • If wall version is easy, try incline push‑up plus. Body straight; small push‑up; at top, protract shoulder blades (the "plus").

Friday — Weights + Elbow Care (~45–50 min)

  • Lean forward; let arm hang relaxed. Start tiny circles from body momentum.
Quadruped Shoulder Flexion — 6 reps/side new exercise
  • Hands/knees; reach one arm forward and slightly up; hold 2s; lower slowly. Maintain neutral spine.
  • Focus on smooth scapular motion; no hiking or winging.
Goblet Squat — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 • 3‑1‑1 tempo
  • KB/DB at chest; 3s down • 1s pause • drive up. Maintain form integrity.
Romanian Deadlift (DB/KB) — 3×6–8 light–moderate
  • Hinge at hips; neutral spine; controlled lowering and lifting.
Chest‑Supported Row (neutral grip) — 3×10–12
  • Focus on scapular retraction; pause at top; slow negative.
Incline Push‑Up Plus — 2–3×10–15
  • Quality over quantity; protract blades at top (plus movement).
Eccentric Wrist Extension — 3×8–12 (3s eccentric)
  • Light weight; focus on 3s lowering phase.
Pronation/Supination — 2–3×15
  • Controlled rotation; pain-free range only.
