Week 8 – Upper Body Finale

Celebrate your progress with classic presses and rows while protecting the shoulder.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Bench Press (Narrow Grip): 3 sets of 10 reps using barbell or dumbbells. Keep elbows close to reduce shoulder stress【112200208061306†L154-L160】.
  • Pull‑Up or Assisted Pull‑Up: 3 sets of 8 reps. Use a band or perform inverted rows if needed.
  • Dumbbell Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps with light weight. Keep core tight and avoid leaning back.
  • Band Pull‑Apart & External Rotation: 3 sets of 15 reps each. Strengthen posterior shoulder muscles and rotator cuff.
  • Dead Bug Variation: 3 sets of 10 reps per side. Keep spine neutral.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
