Morning Strength Recovery Plan

Daily Warm-Up (10 Min)

  • Scapular Push-ups: 2×10 (slow)
  • Wall Slides: 2×10
  • YTWs: 2 rounds of 10 each (Y, T, W)
  • Cat-Cow: 1 min
  • Thoracic Rotations: 10 each side
  • Scapular Rows (bodyweight): 2×10

Monday – Bench + Squat

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • Barbell Bench (rack): 4×5 @60–65%
  • Goblet Squat (20kg KB): 3×10
  • Floor Press (21kg DB): 3×8 paused
  • Reverse Lunges (KB): 2×12 per leg

Tuesday – Deadlift + Rows

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • Deadlift (rack): 4×5
  • Bent-Over Row (21kg DB): 3×10 each side
  • Single-Leg RDL (KB): 3×8 each
  • Rear Delt Raises (bodyweight/KB): 2×15

Wednesday – Arms + Shoulders

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • Shrugs (20kg KB): 3×12
  • Hammer Curls (21kg DB): 3×10
  • Triceps Dips (rack): 3×10
  • YTWs: 2 rounds
  • Wall Press: 2×20s per arm

Thursday – Press + Squat

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • Floor Press (21kg DB): 4×8
  • Front Squat (rack): 4×5
  • Push-Ups: 3×10
  • Shrugs (KB): 2×15 slow

Friday – Posterior + Core

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • Deadlift (lighter): 4×5
  • Bird-Dog Rows (floor): 3×10
  • Glute Bridge (floor): 3×12
  • Plank: 2×1min
  • Rear Delt Raises: 2×12

Saturday – DB Medley + Conditioning

  • Warm-Up Flow
  • DB Floor Press: 3×10
  • Renegade Rows: 3×8 each
  • DB Lateral + Front Raise: 3×12
  • Farmer’s Carry (KB): 3 rounds
  • Bodyweight Squats: 2×15

Sunday – Recovery

  • Walk or Mobility Flow
  • Rehab: wall slides, shoulder rolls
