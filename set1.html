Morning Strength Recovery Plan    

Daily Warm-Up (10 Minutes)

 
   
         
  • Scapular Push-ups – 2 sets of 10 reps (slow and controlled)
    •      
  • Wall Slides – 2 sets of 10 reps (stand with back against wall, slide arms up/down in "goal post" shape)
    •      
  • YTWs – 2 rounds:        
               
    • Y raise – 10 reps (thumbs up, 45° angle from body)
      •          
    • T raise – 10 reps (straight out to sides, squeeze shoulder blades)
      •          
    • W raise – 10 reps (bend elbows, pull into a "W" position)
      •        
         
    •      
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 1 minute (gentle alternation of arch and round back)
    •      
  • Thoracic Rotations – 10 reps per side (on all fours, thread arm under and reach up)
    •      
  • Scapular Rows (using band or slow bent-over motion with no weight) – 2 sets of 10
    •    
 
 

Monday – Bench Rebuild + Squat (Light)

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow (see above)
    •    
  • Barbell Bench – 4 x 5 (start at 60–65%)
    •    
  • Goblet Squat – 3 x 10
    •    
  • Paused DB Bench – 3 x 8
    •    
  • Reverse Lunges – 2 x 12 per leg
    •  
 

Tuesday – Deadlift + Pull

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow (see above)
    •    
  • Barbell or Trap Bar Deadlift – 4 x 5
    •    
  • Chest-supported Row (DB or machine) – 3 x 10
    •    
  • Single-leg RDL – 3 x 8 each side
    •    
  • Face Pulls (or rear delt flys) – 2 x 15
    •  
 

Wednesday – Arms + Shoulder Health

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow (see above)
    •    
  • Slow Shrugs – 3 x 12 @ 20 kg
    •    
  • Hammer Curls – 3 x 10
    •    
  • Triceps Kickbacks or Dips – 3 x 10 (pain-free)
    •    
  • YTWs – 2 rounds
    •    
  • Overhead Isometric Wall Press – 2 x 20s per arm
    •  
 

Thursday – Incline Bench + Front Squat

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow + 5-min light cycle/row
    •    
  • Incline DB Bench (light) – 4 x 8
    •    
  • Front Squat – 4 x 5
    •    
  • Incline Push-up or Machine Press – 3 x 10
    •    
  • DB Shrugs – 2 x 15 slow
    •  
 

Friday – Posterior Chain + Core

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow (see above)
    •    
  • Deadlift – 4 x 5 (lighter than Tuesday)
    •    
  • Bird-Dog Rows or Inverted Rows – 3 x 10
    •    
  • Glute Bridges or Hip Thrusts – 3 x 12
    •    
  • Plank Hold – 2 x 1 min
    •    
  • Rear Delt DB Raises – 2 x 12
    •  
 

Saturday – Dumbbell Medley + Conditioning

 
       
  • Warm-up Flow (see above)
    •    
  • DB Floor Press – 3 x 10
    •    
  • Renegade Rows – 3 x 8 per side
    •    
  • DB Lateral Raise + Front Raise (combo) – 3 x 12
    •    
  • Farmer’s Carry (short, heavy) – 3 rounds
    •    
  • Bodyweight Squats or Step-ups – 2 x 15
    •  
 

Sunday – Recovery

 
       
  • Optional: Walk, mobility flow, or full rest
    •    
  • Optional rehab circuit: wall slides, shoulder rolls, thoracic twist
    •
