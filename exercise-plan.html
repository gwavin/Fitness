Progress & Safety Guidelines

Your health is the priority. Adhere to these rules strictly.
  • Pain scale rule: 0‑2/10 pain is acceptable during/after; >3 = modify the exercise or stop. Do not push through shoulder pain.
  • RPE diary: Jot down the RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) after each main lift. If two sessions in a row feel >8, reduce the load or volume next time.
  • Reload week: If sleep, nutrition, or recovery dips, don't be afraid to repeat a previous, lighter week. Progress isn't always linear.
  • Protein target: Aim for 1.6‑1.8 g/kg bodyweight (~160‑180 g/day) to support muscle repair and growth.
  • Physio check‑in: Book a review with a professional if shoulder soreness lingers for more than 48 hours or if your overhead range of motion plateaus.

After Week 8

If your shoulder tolerates the landmine press and/or dumbbell press at a significant effort (RPE 8) without pain, you can consider re-introducing the barbell overhead press very gradually. A good starting point would be 3 sets of 10 with just the empty bar, adding only 2.5 kg per week and monitoring symptoms closely.
