Your Workout for Today: Monday, 21 July 2025

Here is your exact sequence for this morning's session. Aim for RPE 7-8 (feeling like you have 2-3 good reps left in the tank).

1. DYNAMIC WARM-UP (5 mins)

  • Arm Circles (forward & backward) - 30s each
  • Cat-Camel Stretch - 60s
  • Bodyweight Squats - 15 reps
  • Leg Swings (forward & side-to-side) - 30s each leg
  • Thoracic Rotations (on all fours) - 8 each side

2. MAIN WORK (35 mins)

Perform this as a superset. Do one set of A1, rest 30 seconds, do one set of A2, rest 60-90 seconds. Repeat for 4 total rounds.

A1: Goblet Squat 4 sets of 8 reps (with 20 kg kettlebell)
A2: Side Plank 4 sets of 30 seconds per side

After completing the above, perform the following for 3 sets, resting 60-90 seconds between sets.

B1: Romanian Deadlift (RDL) 3 sets of 10 reps (with dumbbells)

3. COOL-DOWN (5 mins)

  • Deep Squat Hold (prying hips open) - 60s total
  • Couch Stretch (for hip flexors) - 60s per side
  • Lying Hamstring Stretch - 60s per side

4. TONIGHT'S OPTIONAL MOBILITY

  • Focus: Shoulder band routine (see Evening Mobility Menu below for examples like Banded ER/IR).

Key Principles

ThemeApplication
Re‑build shoulder capacity cautiouslyEarly focus on horizontal pushes/pulls, rotator‑cuff & scapular control; only light, high‑rep overhead work after Week 3.
Progressive overload, not over‑reachIncrease either load or total reps ≈ 5‑10 % every 2 weeks—never both at once. Cap sets at RPE 7‑8 (2‑3 reps “in the tank”).
Session density, not lengthPair lifts as supersets; rest 60‑90 s between paired moves to finish in ≤ 45 min.

Progress & Safety Guidelines

Your health is the priority. Adhere to these rules strictly.
  • Pain scale rule: 0‑2/10 pain is acceptable during/after; >3 = modify the exercise or stop. Do not push through shoulder pain.
  • RPE diary: Jot down the RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) after each main lift. If two sessions in a row feel >8, reduce the load or volume next time.
  • Reload week: If sleep, nutrition, or recovery dips, don't be afraid to repeat a previous, lighter week. Progress isn't always linear.
  • Protein target: Aim for 1.6‑1.8 g/kg bodyweight (~160‑180 g/day) to support muscle repair and growth.
  • Physio check‑in: Book a review with a professional if shoulder soreness lingers for more than 48 hours or if your overhead range of motion plateaus.

After Week 8

If your shoulder tolerates the landmine press and/or dumbbell press at a significant effort (RPE 8) without pain, you can consider re-introducing the barbell overhead press very gradually. A good starting point would be 3 sets of 10 with just the empty bar, adding only 2.5 kg per week and monitoring symptoms closely.
