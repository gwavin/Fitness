Barbell Complex Workout

Instructions:

  • Perform all exercises back-to-back without setting the bar down.
  • Use the same weight for all exercises (start with 50kg).
  • Rest for 60-90 seconds between rounds.
  • Begin with 3 rounds and increase rounds or weight as you progress.

Workout:

  • Deadlift: 5 reps
  • Bent-Over Row: 5 reps
  • Hang Clean: 5 reps
  • Front Squat: 5 reps
  • Push Press: 5 reps
  • Back Squat: 5 reps

Progression Tips:

  • Week 1: 3 rounds, 50kg, 60-90 seconds rest.
  • Week 2: 4 rounds, 50kg, reduce rest to 60 seconds.
  • Week 3: 4-5 rounds, add weight (55kg), 90 seconds rest.

Focus on maintaining perfect form throughout. Reduce weight if needed.
