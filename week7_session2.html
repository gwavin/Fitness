Week 7 – Upper Body Strength Challenge

Combine pressing and rowing with core stabilisation work.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Floor Press: 3 sets of 8–10 reps using barbell or dumbbells. Keep elbows at 45°.
  • Renegade Row: 3 sets of 10 reps per side holding dumbbells. Maintain a plank while rowing each dumbbell.
  • Band Face Pull: 3 sets of 15 reps. Pull band towards face and externally rotate shoulders.
  • Bear Crawl: 3 sets of 30‑second crawls. Keep knees low and move opposite hand and foot together.
  • Dead Bug Variation: 3 sets of 10 reps per side. Extend opposite arm and leg while keeping core engaged.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
