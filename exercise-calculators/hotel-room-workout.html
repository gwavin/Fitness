Hotel Room Workout

Shoulder-friendly routine, roughly 30–40 minutes. Only push-up stands and bodyweight required.

Warm-Up (5 min)

  • Arm Circles – 1 min each direction
  • Cat-Cow Stretches – 1 min
  • Scapular Push-Ups – 2×10

Strength Circuit (20–25 min)

Complete 3–4 rounds. Rest about 60 s between exercises.

  • Push-Up Stand Push-Ups – 8–12 reps, elbows tucked
  • Split Squats – 10 reps each leg
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps – 10 taps per side
  • Glute Bridge – 15 reps
  • Wall Angels – 10 slow reps

Core & Mobility Finisher (5–10 min)

  • Side Plank – 30 s each side
  • Reverse Snow Angels – 2×10
  • Child’s Pose with Reach – hold 30 s each side

Adjust reps if needed and stop if shoulder pain increases.
