Week 2 – Full‑Body Circuit

Blend kettlebell and dumbbell exercises for a metabolic workout without changing weights.

Warm‑Up

Begin with 5–7 minutes of low‑impact activity like brisk walking or cycling followed by dynamic stretches for the shoulders and hips (arm circles, leg swings). Warming up increases circulation and prepares the muscles for exercise; research from the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons suggests a 5–10‑minute low‑impact warm up before strengthening exercises and stretching afterwards【963587572219979†L210-L217】.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Kettlebell Deadlift: 3 sets of 12 reps. Hinge at hips and keep back flat.
  • Front Squat (Goblet): 3 sets of 10 reps holding a dumbbell or kettlebell close to your chest.
  • Dumbbell RDL to Row: 3 sets of 10 reps. Perform a Romanian deadlift then a row at the bottom of the movement.
  • Farmer’s Carry: 3 sets of 30‑second carries. Hold a dumbbell or kettlebell in each hand and walk with good posture.
  • Bicycle Crunch: 3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side). Keep lower back pressed into the floor.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness【963587572219979†L210-L217】.
