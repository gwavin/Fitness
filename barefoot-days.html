8-Week Barefoot Add‑On Plan

This schedule expands on the barefoot notes shown in the workout calendar. It assumes you already wear minimalist shoes and want to add short barefoot jogs on rest days.

WeekRest Day Activity
1On two rest days, warm up with a 5 min walk, then jog 200 m (or ~1 min) on soft grass. Walk back to cool down.
2Jog 3 × 1 min on two rest days, walking a minute between runs. Add a short barefoot walk on another rest day.
3Try 3 × 2 min jogs (or ~400 m each) on soft ground. Continue one easy barefoot walk.
4Finish a 20 min walk with 4 × 1 min runs. Keep recovery walks brief.
5Two run/walk sessions: 6–8 min of easy running total. Add one extra 20 min walk.
6Run 10–12 min continuously on soft surface once this week. On a second day, run 5–8 min, including a short 100–200 m stretch on smooth pavement.
7One run of 15–20 min on grass or trail. Another run of 8–10 min can include up to 0.5 mi on pavement.
8Target a 2 mi run on soft surface and a separate 1 mi run on pavement, focusing on light steps and good form.

Always listen to your feet. If anything feels painful, take extra rest and repeat the previous week.
