Gentle 4-Week Exercise Reintroduction Plan

Current plan for rebuilding consistency and tissue tolerance around Monday/Thursday Shotokan karate. Use RPE 5-6, stop with 4-5 good reps in reserve, and finish sessions with fuel left in the tank. This is personal training guidance, not medical advice.

Week 1 - Restart

Week 1

Use 2 sets for most exercises. Keep everything easy. Prioritise movement quality. No soreness hunting.

First-month ceiling
  • No max testing, grinders, HIIT, sprinting, or heavy loading.
  • Karate already provides impact, pivots, sudden loading, balance demands, and skill work.
