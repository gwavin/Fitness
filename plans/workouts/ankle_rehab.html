Ankle Rehab & Stretching

Use this simple routine on rest days or after workouts to improve ankle mobility and strengthen supporting muscles.

Rehab Exercises

  • Ankle Circles: 2 sets of 10 each direction.
  • Ankle Alphabet: Trace the letters Aâ€“Z with your toes.
  • Resistance Band Dorsiflexion: 2 sets of 15 reps.
  • Resistance Band Plantarflexion: 2 sets of 15 reps.

Stretching

  • Calf Stretch: Hold 30 seconds per side.
  • Soleus Stretch: Bend the back knee and hold 30 seconds per side.
  • Plantar Fascia Stretch: Pull toes back gently and hold 30 seconds per side.
