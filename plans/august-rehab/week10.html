Week 10 Defaults
  • Main Lifts: Consider transitioning toward barbell movements if shoulder stability excellent. Continue 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7.
  • Upper Integration: Complex movement patterns; Push‑Up Plus (floor) 3×8–15; overhead work progression.
  • Elbow: Eccentric wrist extension 3×12–18 + comprehensive forearm strengthening complex.
  • Run sessions: 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) between 5′ warm‑up/cool‑down (≈20 min jog total). Longer intervals, fewer reps.
  • Focus on movement quality under fatigue and preparing for return to normal training patterns.

Week 10 – Alternating Days (Weights ↔ Run) + Daily Shoulder Rehab

Integrated strength training with focus on movement quality under fatigue. Prepare for return to normal activity patterns. Use buttons to expand/collapse details.

Monday — Integrated Strength Training (~65–70 min)

Movement Preparation Complex — 2 rounds comprehensive warm-up
  • 10 arm circles each direction + 5 shoulder rolls + 8 neck rotations.
  • 10 leg swings each direction + 10 hip circles each way.
  • 8 cat-cow + 6 thoracic rotations each side.
Handstand + Hollow Hold Complex — 2×25s handstand + 20s hollow hold core integration
  • Wall handstand hold followed immediately by hollow body hold.
  • Shoulder stability + core strength integration.
  • Regress handstand to incline if needed; skip if any shoulder discomfort.
Back Squat (bodyweight → barbell) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 potential progression
  • If shoulder stable with load overhead, explore bodyweight or light barbell back squat.
  • Only progress if completely comfortable; goblet version remains excellent option.
Romanian Deadlift (barbell exploration) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Progress toward barbell RDL if grip and shoulder stability excellent.
  • Focus on hip hinge pattern mastery regardless of implement.
Superset: Row + Overhead Press — 3×8–10 each opposing movement superset
  • Chest-supported row immediately followed by overhead press.
  • Challenges shoulder stability in opposing movement patterns.
Push-Up Complex — 3 rounds: 8 floor plus + 5 pike + 5 single-arm (each) advanced complex
  • Floor push-up plus → pike push-ups → single-arm push-ups (modify as needed).
  • Progressive challenge; maintain quality throughout.
Turkish Get-Up — 3×2/side light to moderate full movement
  • Work toward full Turkish get-up if comfortable; ultimate shoulder stability exercise.
  • Light to moderate weight; focus on control and stability.
  • Break into segments if needed; skip if any discomfort.
Loaded Carry Medley — 3×50m mixed carries comprehensive carry
  • Farmer's walk 20m + suitcase carry 15m each side + overhead carry 15m.
  • Comprehensive loading patterns; moderate weight.
Complete Forearm + Grip Complex — 3×15 each exercise
  • Eccentric wrist extension + pronation/supination + grip strengthening + finger extension + wrist flexor stretch.

Tuesday — Extended Intervals + Dynamic Lower Leg + Daily Shoulder Rehab (~60 min)

Foot + Ankle Complex — 3 rounds comprehensive preparation
  • Short-foot drill 40s each side + ankle circles 10 each direction.
  • Calf stretch 30s each + tibialis activation 15 reps.
Single-Leg Strength + Plyometric Prep — 3×15/side + 15 hops dynamic progression
  • Single-leg calf raises followed by small hops in place.
  • Progress to single-leg hops if stable; focus on landing control.
Multi-Directional Ankle Stability — 3×20 + 8 steps each direction
  • Tibialis raises + forward/backward/lateral steps.
  • Comprehensive ankle stability in all planes.
Run – Week 10 Session A — 5' walk → 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) → 5' walk extended intervals
  • 4-minute intervals require pacing discipline; start conservatively; build rhythm.
  • If 4-minute intervals too challenging, reduce to 3.5 minutes; progression is gradual.
Dynamic Scapular Control — 3×10 movement under challenge
  • Scapular setting while walking in place; control under movement challenge.
Resistance Band Flow — 2×8 (continuous flow) flowing movement
  • Pull-apart → external rotation → high pull → overhead → return; continuous movement.
Wall Mobility + Reach Complex — 3×8 reaching under load
  • Wall slide + angels + overhead reach + lateral reach; multi-planar movement.
Advanced Push‑Up Plus — 2×10–15
  • Floor version with strong protraction; focus on stability under fatigue.

Wednesday — Integrated Strength Training (~65–70 min)

Movement Preparation Complex — 2 rounds
  • Comprehensive warm-up; prepare body for integrated training session.
Bear Crawl + Crab Walk — 2×15 steps each locomotion patterns
  • Bear crawl forward/backward + crab walk side to side.
  • Shoulder stability in various positions and movement patterns.
Back Squat (progression) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Continue squat progression; barbell version if shoulder stability allows.
Romanian Deadlift (barbell focus) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Barbell RDL progression; hip hinge pattern under load.
Push-Pull Superset — 3×6–8 each heavy superset
  • Chest-supported row + overhead press; moderate to heavy loading.
  • Focus on maintaining form under higher loads.
Single-Arm Work Complex — 2×6 each arm unilateral challenge
  • Single-arm row + single-arm overhead press; unilateral stability challenge.
  • Core and shoulder stability under asymmetric loading.
Turkish Get-Up — 3×2/side moderate
  • Continue TGU progression; moderate weight; control throughout range.
Metabolic Finisher — 3 rounds: 30s work/15s rest conditioning integration
  • Mountain climbers + plank hold + jumping jacks.
  • Maintain shoulder stability under metabolic stress.
Complete Forearm Complex — 3×15–18 each
  • Comprehensive forearm strengthening; slight progression available.

Thursday — Extended Intervals + Dynamic Lower Leg + Daily Shoulder Rehab (~60 min)

Foot + Ankle Complex — 3 rounds
  • Comprehensive foot and ankle preparation.
Single-Leg Strength + Plyometrics — 3×15/side + 15 hops
  • Strength followed by dynamic preparation; progress as tolerated.
Multi-Directional Stability — 3×20 + 8 steps each direction
  • Comprehensive ankle stability work.
Run – Week 10 Session B — 5' walk → 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) → 5' walk
  • Focus on consistent pacing; sustainable effort throughout all 4-minute intervals.
  • Monitor effort and recovery; adjust if needed for proper adaptation.
Dynamic Scapular Control — 3×10
  • Scapular control under movement challenge.
Resistance Band Flow — 2×8 continuous
  • Flowing movement pattern; smooth transitions throughout.
Wall Mobility + Reach Complex — 3×8
  • Multi-planar shoulder mobility and stability.
Advanced Push‑Up Plus — 2×10–15
  • Floor version; maintain quality under fatigue.

Friday — Peak Integrated Training Session (~65–70 min)

Complete Movement Preparation — 3 rounds thorough warm-up
  • Joint mobility + dynamic stretching + activation exercises.
  • Prepare for peak training session.
Complex Movement Pattern — 3×5 advanced integration
  • Squat → overhead press → lunge → row → Turkish get-up (partial).
  • Light weight; focus on movement quality and transitions.
  • Break into components if too challenging; progress gradually.
Heavy Back Squat — 3×5–6 @ RPE 7 intensity increase
  • If shoulder stable, progress to slightly higher intensity; form priority.
Heavy Romanian Deadlift — 3×5–6 @ RPE 7
  • Progress intensity slightly; maintain perfect hip hinge pattern.
Push-Pull Circuit — 3 rounds: 45s work/15s rest time-based circuit
  • Chest-supported row → overhead press → push-up plus → face pulls.
  • Maintain form under time pressure.
Turkish Get-Up Ladder — 1-2-1 each side volume progression
  • 1 TGU each side, then 2, then 1; moderate weight.
  • Ultimate shoulder stability under increasing volume.
Comprehensive Carry Medley — 4×60m mixed
  • Farmer's walk + suitcase carry + overhead carry + front-loaded carry.
  • Complete loading pattern challenge.
Advanced Forearm Complex — 4×12–15 each
  • Peak volume for forearm strengthening complex.

  • Thorough preparation for extended running session.

  • Prepare for dynamic loading patterns.
Multi-Directional Stability — 3×20 + 10 steps each direction volume increase
  • Peak ankle stability work.
Run – Week 10 Session C — 5' walk → 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) → 5' walk
  • Focus on rhythm and consistency; enjoy the longer intervals.
  • This is peak running volume; listen to body and adjust as needed.
Dynamic Scapular Control — 3×12 volume increase
  • Extended scapular control under movement.
Advanced Resistance Band Flow — 3×8 continuous set increase
  • Add third set; maintain flow throughout.
Complex Wall Work — 3×10 rep increase
  • Multi-planar wall mobility complex; peak range work.
Advanced Push‑Up Plus — 3×12–15 volume increase
  • Peak volume; maintain quality throughout all sets.

  • Compare to Week 1; note improvements in range, strength, and comfort.
  • Gentle testing of shoulder ranges that were previously limited.
  • Document any remaining limitations for final weeks.

  • Light maintenance work; recovery focus.

  • Very light resistance; movement maintenance only.
