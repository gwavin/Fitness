Week 10 Defaults
  • Main Lifts: Consider transitioning toward barbell movements if shoulder stability excellent. Continue 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7.
  • Upper Integration: Complex movement patterns; Push‑Up Plus (floor) 3×8–15; overhead work progression.
  • Elbow: Eccentric wrist extension 3×12–18 + comprehensive forearm strengthening complex.
  • Run sessions: 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) between 5′ warm‑up/cool‑down (≈20 min jog total). Longer intervals, fewer reps.
  • Focus on movement quality under fatigue and preparing for return to normal training patterns.

Week 10 – Alternating Days (Weights ↔ Run) + Daily Shoulder Rehab

Monday — Integrated Strength Training (~65–70 min)

Movement Preparation Complex — 2 rounds comprehensive warm-up
  • Arm Circles: Stand tall. Extend arms out to sides. Perform 10 small circles forward, then 10 backward, gradually increasing size.
  • Shoulder Rolls: Shrug shoulders up to ears, roll them back and down. 5 reps.
  • Neck Rotations: Gently look right/left, and tilt ear to shoulder. 8 reps total.
  • Leg Swings: Swing one leg forward/backward 10 times, then side-to-side 10 times. Switch legs.
  • Hip Circles: Hands on hips, rotate hips in large circles. 10 each direction.
  • Cat-Cow: On hands and knees. Inhale/drop belly (Cow), Exhale/round spine (Cat). 8 reps.
  • Thoracic Rotations: In tabletop, hand behind head. Rotate elbow to opposite wrist, then up to ceiling. 6 reps/side.
Handstand + Hollow Hold Complex — 2×25s handstand + 20s hollow hold core integration
  • Wall Handstand: Kick up or walk feet up wall. Press floor away firmly. Keep ribs knitted down (don't arch). Hold 25s.
  • Hollow Hold: Immediately after, lie on back. Lift legs and shoulders/head off floor. Press lower back into ground. Arms overhead or by sides. Hold 20s.
  • Regress handstand to box pike hold or tall plank if shoulder discomfort exists.
Back Squat (bodyweight → barbell) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 potential progression
  • Setup: Barbell on upper back traps (or goblet hold). Feet shoulder-width. Brace core.
  • Action: Push hips back and bend knees. Keep chest tall. Descent until thighs parallel to floor.
  • Return: Drive through mid-foot to stand. Squeeze glutes.
Romanian Deadlift (barbell exploration) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Setup: Feet hip-width. Hold bar in front of thighs. Shoulders back.
  • Action: Hinge at hips, pushing butt back. Keep knees soft but fixed. Bar slides down thighs.
  • Depth: Go until hamstring stretch is felt (usually mid-shin). Keep back flat.
  • Return: Drive hips forward to stand tall.
Superset: Row + Overhead Press — 3×8–10 each opposing movement superset
  • Chest-Supported Row: Lie face down on incline bench. Row dumbbells to hips, squeezing shoulder blades. 8-10 reps.
  • Overhead Press: Immediately stand/sit. Press dumbbells from shoulders to overhead lockout. Keep core tight. 8-10 reps.
Push-Up Complex — 3 rounds: 8 floor plus + 5 pike + 5 single-arm (each) advanced complex
  • Push-Up Plus (8 reps): Standard push-up position. At top, push floor away to spread shoulder blades (protraction). Sink chest slightly (retraction). Keep elbows straight.
  • Pike Push-Up (5 reps): Downward dog position (hips high). Lower head to floor, press back up.
  • Single-Arm Push-Up (5/side): Incline or wall if needed. One arm behind back. Lower chest, press up.
Turkish Get-Up — 3×2/side light to moderate full movement
  • Sequence: 1. Roll to elbow. 2. Push to hand. 3. Bridge hips. 4. Sweep leg back to knee. 5. Lunge up to stand.
  • Reverse: Lunge down → Hand down → Sweep leg → Elbow → Back.
  • Keep arm locked and eyes on weight throughout.
  • Break into segments if needed; skip if any discomfort.
Loaded Carry Medley — 3×50m mixed carries comprehensive carry
  • Farmer's Walk (20m): Heavy weights in both hands. Shoulders back. Walk fast but controlled.
  • Suitcase Carry (15m): Weight in one hand. Resist leaning. Switch hands at 15m.
  • Overhead Carry (15m): Weight pressed overhead. Ribs down. Walk controlled.
Complete Forearm + Grip Complex — 3×15 each exercise
  • Eccentric Wrist Extension: Forearm on bench. Lift weight with help, lower slowly (5s) with one hand. 15 reps.
  • Pronation/Supination: Rotate forearm palm-up to palm-down with hammer/weight. 15 reps.
  • Grip Crush: Squeeze ball/gripper. 15 reps.
  • Finger Extension: Expand rubber band with fingers. 15 reps.

Tuesday — Extended Intervals + Dynamic Lower Leg + Daily Shoulder Rehab (~60 min)

Foot + Ankle Complex — 3 rounds comprehensive preparation
  • Short-Foot Drill (40s): Stand barefoot. Pull big toe towards heel to arch foot without curling toes. Hold tension.
  • Ankle Circles (10 each): Full range rotations.
  • Calf Stretch (30s): Heel down, leg straight/bent.
  • Tibialis Activation (15 reps): Lean against wall, lift toes to shins.
Single-Leg Strength + Plyometric Prep — 3×15/side + 15 hops dynamic progression
  • Single-Leg Calf Raise: Stand on one leg. Lift heel high, lower slowly. 15 reps.
  • Hops: Small, rhythmic hops in place. stiff ankles, quick ground contact. 15 reps.
Multi-Directional Ankle Stability — 3×20 + 8 steps each direction
  • Tibialis Raises (20 reps): Lean back on wall, lift toes.
  • 4-Way Reach (8 reps): Stand on one leg. Reach other foot Forward-Side-Back-Behind. Tap floor lightly and return.
Run – Week 10 Session A — 5' walk → 5× (4min jog / 90s walk) → 5' walk extended intervals
  • Warm-up: 5 min brisk walk.
  • Intervals: Jog 4 minutes, Walk 90 seconds. Repeat 5 times.
  • Cool-down: 5 min walk.
  • Focus: Pacing discipline. Start slower than you think.
  • If 4-minute intervals too challenging, reduce to 3.5 minutes.
Dynamic Scapular Control — 3×10 movement under challenge
  • Action: March in place (high knees). While marching, retract and depress scapula (squeeze back and down).
  • Hold: Maintain squeeze for 3-5 seconds while legs continue moving. Release. 10 reps.
Resistance Band Flow — 2×8 (continuous flow) flowing movement
  • Sequence: 1. Pull-Apart (chest level) → 2. External Rotation (elbows at sides) → 3. High Pull (face pull) → 4. Overhead Pass/Press → Return.
  • Perform as one continuous, smooth repetition.
Wall Mobility + Reach Complex — 3×8 reaching under load
  • Wall Slide: Back to wall, slide arms W to Y. Keep contact.
  • Angels: Snow angel motion against wall.
  • Overhead Reach: Face wall, slide hands up high.
  • Lateral Reach: Side to wall, reach arm over head to wall.
Advanced Push‑Up Plus — 2×10–15
  • Setup: Standard push-up position (toes and hands).
  • Action: Keep elbows straight. Push floor away to dome upper back (protraction). Drop chest between shoulders (retraction).
  • Focus on range of motion at shoulder blade.

Wednesday — Integrated Strength Training (~65–70 min)

Movement Preparation Complex — 2 rounds
  • Arm Circles: 10 forward/10 backward.
  • Shoulder Rolls: 5 reps.
  • Neck Rotations: 8 reps.
  • Leg Swings: 10 forward-back, 10 side-side.
  • Hip Circles: 10 each way.
  • Cat-Cow: 8 reps.
  • Thoracic Rotations: 6 reps/side.
Bear Crawl + Crab Walk — 2×15 steps each locomotion patterns
  • Bear Crawl: Hands and toes, knees hovering. Opposite hand and foot move together. Keep back flat. 15 steps.
  • Crab Walk: Sit, lift hips (reverse table). Walk hands and feet. Open chest. 15 steps.
Back Squat (progression) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Setup: Barbell on traps or Goblet. Feet shoulder-width.
  • Action: Hips back, knees bend. Chest up. Depth to parallel.
  • Return: Drive up. Maintain core brace.
Romanian Deadlift (barbell focus) — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7
  • Setup: Barbell in hands. Feet hip-width.
  • Action: Hinge hips back. Bar slides down thighs. Keep lats engaged (bar close).
  • Range: Stretch hamstrings without rounding back. Drive hips forward to finish.
Push-Pull Superset — 3×6–8 each heavy superset
  • Chest-Supported Row: 6-8 heavy reps. Squeeze back.
  • Overhead Press: 6-8 reps. Solid lockout. Core tight.
  • Perform back-to-back with minimal rest.
Single-Arm Work Complex — 2×6 each arm unilateral challenge
  • Single-Arm Row: Hand on bench. Row dumbbell to hip. Resist twisting. 6 reps.
  • Single-Arm OH Press: Stand/Sit. Press one dumbbell overhead. Engage obliques to stay upright. 6 reps.
Turkish Get-Up — 3×2/side moderate
  • Execution: Roll → Press → Bridge → Sweep → Lunge → Stand.
  • Focus: Smooth transitions. Keep eye on the weight.
Metabolic Finisher — 3 rounds: 30s work/15s rest conditioning integration
  • Mountain Climbers (30s): Plank position. Drive knees to chest alternating fast.
  • Plank Hold (30s): Elbows under shoulders. Squeeze glutes and abs.
  • Jumping Jacks (30s): Standard jacks.
Complete Forearm Complex — 3×15–18 each
  • Wrist Extension (Eccentric): 15-18 reps. Slow lower.
  • Rotation: 15-18 reps.
  • Grip Squeeze: 15-18 reps.
  • Finger Open: 15-18 reps.

Friday — Peak Integrated Training Session (~65–70 min)

Complete Movement Preparation — 3 rounds thorough warm-up
  • Arm Circles: Stand tall. Extend arms. 10 circles forward/backward.
  • Shoulder Rolls: Shrug, roll back and down. 5 reps.
  • Neck Rotations: Gentle look left/right. 8 reps.
  • Leg Swings: 10 forward/backward, 10 side-to-side.
  • Hip Circles: Large rotations. 10 each way.
  • Cat-Cow: Hands/knees. Inhale drop belly, exhale round spine. 8 reps.
  • Thoracic Rotations: Tabletop, elbow to wrist then ceiling. 6 reps/side.
Complex Movement Pattern — 3×5 advanced integration
  • The Complex: Holding light weights:
  • 1. Squat (full depth).
  • 2. Overhead Press (at top of squat).
  • 3. Reverse Lunge (one each leg, weights overhead or racked).
  • 4. RDL/Hinge to Row (one row).
  • 5. Partial TGU (roll to elbow and back).
  • Repeat sequence 5 times.
  • Break into components if too challenging.
Heavy Back Squat — 3×5–6 @ RPE 7 intensity increase
  • Setup: Barbell rack position. Solid brace.
  • Action: Controlled descent. Explosive drive up.
  • RPE 7: Challenging, but 3 reps in reserve.
Heavy Romanian Deadlift — 3×5–6 @ RPE 7
  • Action: Strict hip hinge. Heavy load.
  • Focus: Hamstring tension. Flat back. Lats tight.
Push-Pull Circuit — 3 rounds: 45s work/15s rest time-based circuit
  • 1. Chest-Supported Row: 45s reps.
  • 2. Overhead Press: 45s reps.
  • 3. Push-Up Plus: 45s reps.
  • 4. Face Pulls: Pull band/cable to face, separate hands. 45s reps.
Turkish Get-Up Ladder — 1-2-1 each side volume progression
  • Ladder: Perform 1 rep Left, 1 rep Right.
  • Then 2 reps Left, 2 reps Right.
  • Then 1 rep Left, 1 rep Right.
  • Rest as needed between rungs.
Comprehensive Carry Medley — 4×60m mixed
  • 1. Farmer's: 60m (Both hands).
  • 2. Suitcase: 30m Left / 30m Right.
  • 3. Overhead: 30m Left / 30m Right.
  • 4. Front-Loaded: Hold weights at shoulders (rack). 60m.
Advanced Forearm Complex — 4×12–15 each
  • Eccentric Wrist Extension: Forearm on bench. Lower slowly. 12-15 reps.
  • Pronation/Supination: Rotate forearm with weight. 12-15 reps.
  • Grip Crush: Squeeze ball/gripper. 12-15 reps.
  • Finger Extension: Expand rubber band. 12-15 reps.
