Week 4 — Shoulder + Elbow Rehab with Barefoot Run

Consolidate + small bump; prep for Phase 2
How to use this page
  • Click any exercise to open the full how‑to, cues, mistakes, and regressions/progressions.
  • Pain rule: 0–3/10 is acceptable. Stop for sharp/catching pain or if soreness >24h next day.
  • Week 4 progression: keep main lifts at 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 and add a tiny bump—either +1 rep on one or two sets or a micro‑load if reps are already at the top end. Eccentrics continue for elbow. Run intervals lengthen slightly.
Week 4 Defaults
  • Goblet Squat, RDL: 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7. If Monday is clean next day, add +1 rep to the first set on Wed/Fri or micro‑load 2.5 kg.
  • Upper add‑ins: Chest‑Supported Row (neutral) 3×10–12; Incline Push‑Up Plus 2–3×8–12 (slightly lower the incline if form is crisp).
  • Elbow: continue Eccentric wrist extension 3×8–10 (3s down) + pronation/supination 2–3×12. Keep a single isometric “calm set” handy for flare control (instructions included below).
  • Run sessions: 6× (90s jog / 60s walk) between 5′ warm‑up/cool‑down (≈9 min jog total). Repeat volume if next‑morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min.
  • Grip neutral where possible; straps allowed on hinge work if elbow talks.

Week 4 – Alternating Days (Weights ↔ Run) + Daily Shoulder Rehab

Keep shoulder‑safe angles and cues. Progress only when next‑day symptoms are ≤3/10. Use the buttons below to expand/collapse details.

Monday — Weights + Elbow Care (~40–45 min)

Pendulum Swings — 30s each direction
  • Lean forward, hand on a table/chair. Let the affected arm hang completely relaxed.
  • Start tiny circles from body momentum, not the shoulder muscles. Breathe slowly.
  • Clockwise/counter‑clockwise, then gentle forward/back and side/side if comfortable.
Open Books (T‑spine) — 6 reps/side
  • Side‑lying; hips/knees 90°. Reach top hand up, then open toward the floor behind you while knees stay stacked.
  • Breathe out into the range; no pinching.
Goblet Squat — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 • 3‑1‑1 tempo
  • KB/DB at chest; feet shoulder‑width; brace; sit between hips; chest tall; knees track over mid‑foot.
  • 3s down • 1s pause • drive up over mid‑foot. Heels stay down.
Romanian Deadlift (DB/KB) — 3×6–8 light–moderate
  • Soft knees; hinge hips back; spine neutral; lats packed; weights skim thighs; stop mid‑shin; 2s up. Straps ok if elbow talks.
Chest‑Supported Row (neutral grip) — 3×10–12
  • Bench 30–45°; chest down; palms facing. Depress/retract blades slightly; row elbows 30–45° from body; pause 1s; lower 2–3s. Neck long; no shrug.
  • Cranking with biceps; shoulders hiking; elbows at 90° abduction.
Incline Push‑Up Plus — 2–3×8–12
  • Hands on bench/box/wall; body straight; ribs down. Lower with elbows 30–45°; at top, protract shoulder blades (“plus”) without shrugging.
  • Lower the incline slightly this week if pain‑free and form is crisp.
Eccentric Wrist Extension — 3×8–10 (3s down)
  • Forearm supported, palm down. Use the other hand to help lift; lower 3s under control; painless to mildly sore (≤3/10).
Pronation/Supination (hammer) — 2–3×12 light
  • Hold hammer/DB vertical; rotate to palm‑down then palm‑up in a controlled, pain‑free range. Elbow at 90° by side.
Wrist Extensor Isometric (calm set) — 1×20–30s @ 3–4/10 (use only if flared)
  • Setup: Forearm on table, palm down, wrist just off edge; elbow ~90° tucked; shoulder relaxed.
  • Hold: With the other hand, press gently on back of knuckles and match the pressure so the wrist doesn’t move.
  • Breathe: 20–30s; rest 45–60s. One calm set is usually enough.
  • Angle: Neutral or ~15–20° extension—pick the most comfortable.
  • Alt: Back‑of‑hand press into wall with elbow at 90° for same 20–30s hold.
  • Stop if sharp/nerve pain, tingling, or if pain >3/10 during/next day.

Tuesday — Barefoot Walk–Jog + Foot/Calf Prep (~25–30 min)

Short‑Foot Drill — 3×20s/side
  • Gently lift arch (ball of big toe draws toward heel) without curling toes; breathe and relax the ankle.
Double‑Leg Calf Raise — 3×12 slow
  • Full range up/down 2–3s each; rise over big/second toe; pause briefly at top; no bouncing.
Tibialis Raise — 2×15
  • Back to wall; heels down; lift toes toward shins; control the lower; keep knees soft.
Run – Week 4 Session A — 5’ walk → 6× (90s jog / 60s walk) → 5’ walk longer bouts
  • Cadence ~170–180; land under center; quiet feet; posture tall with slight forward lean from ankles.
  • If next‑morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min, repeat this volume before progressing.
Scapular Setting — 5s ×10
  • Gentle shrug → roll back → set blades down into back pockets; neck relaxed; breathe. Think wide collarbones; no pinching between blades.
Band Internal Rotation — 2×12–15 (very light, 3s return)
  • Band anchored at elbow height; elbow glued at 90° by side. Pull forearm toward belly; pause; slow 3s return. Ribs down, chest tall.
  • Elbow drifting away; shoulder rolling forward.
Wall Angels — 2×8
  • Back/head/butt to wall; ribs gently down. Slide arms up only before winging or back arch; smooth breathing.

Regress: Seated or smaller range. Progress: 2–3s pause at top without shrug.

Push‑Up Plus (wall) — 1×12
  • Hands on wall; body straight. Small push‑up; at the top, actively protract shoulder blades (the “plus”). Neck long; no rib flare.

Chest‑Supported Row (neutral) — 3×10–12
