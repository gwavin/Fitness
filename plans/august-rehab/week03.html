  • Week 3 progression: small volume bump to 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 on main lifts; add Chest‑Supported Row and Incline Push‑Up Plus; begin eccentric wrist extension.
Week 3 Defaults
  • Goblet Squat, RDL: 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7. Technique first; add reps before load.
  • Upper add‑ins: Chest‑Supported Row (neutral) 3×10–12; Incline Push‑Up Plus 2×10–12.
  • Elbow: Eccentric wrist extension 3×8–10 (3s down), + light pronation/supination 2×12. Keep isometrics for flare control only.
  • Run sessions: 8× (60s jog / 60s walk) between 5′ warm‑up/cool‑down.
  • Grip neutral where possible; straps allowed on hinge work if elbow talks.

Week 3 – Alternating Days (Weights ↔ Run) + Daily Shoulder Rehab

Keep shoulder‑safe angles and cues. Progress only when next‑day symptoms are ≤3/10. Use the buttons below to expand/collapse details.

Monday — Weights + Elbow Care (~40–45 min)

Pendulum Swings — 30s each direction
  • Lean forward, hand on a table/chair. Let the affected arm hang completely relaxed.
  • Start tiny circles from your body momentum, not the shoulder muscles. Breathe slowly.
  • Do clockwise/counter‑clockwise, then gentle forward/back and side/side if comfortable.
Open Books (T‑spine) — 6 reps/side
  • Lie on your side, hips/knees at 90°. Arms straight in front, palms together.
  • Reach top hand to the ceiling, then rotate it open toward the floor behind you.
  • Keep knees touching and stacked; let chest/ribs rotate. Breathe out into the stretch.
  • Return with control. Stop short of any pinching.
Goblet Squat — 3×6–8 @ RPE 6–7 • 3‑1‑1 tempo +1 set
  • Hold KB/DB at chest; feet shoulder‑width; brace; sit between hips; chest tall; knees over mid‑foot.
  • 3s down • 1s pause • drive up over mid‑foot. Heels stay down.
Romanian Deadlift (DB/KB) — 3×6–8 light–moderate +1 set
  • Soft knees; hinge by pushing hips back; spine neutral; lats packed.
  • Weights skim thighs; stop mid‑shin; 2s up. Use straps if elbow/grip protests.
Chest‑Supported Row (neutral grip) — 3×10–12
  • Set bench to 30–45°. Lie chest‑down; hold DBs neutral (palms facing).
  • Draw shoulder blades down and slightly together (depress/retract), then row elbows ~30–45° from body.
  • Pause 1s at ribs; lower under control (2–3s). Keep neck long; no shrugging.
  • Cranking with biceps; shoulders hiking up; elbow drifting to 90° abduction.
Incline Push‑Up Plus — 2×10–12 @ easy–moderate
  • Hands on bench/box/wall; body straight; ribs down. Lower with elbows ~30–45° from sides.
  • At the top, add the “plus”: gently protract shoulder blades (push the surface away) without shrugging.
  • Choose an incline that keeps pain ≤3/10 and form clean; adjust height as needed.
Eccentric Wrist Extension — 3×8–10 (3s down)
  • Forearm supported, palm down, wrist free off edge. Use the other hand to help lift the DB, then slowly lower 3s back to start.
  • Range: pain‑free to mildly sore (≤3/10). Rest ~45–60s.
Pronation/Supination (hammer) — 2×12 light
  • Hold a hammer/DB vertical (thumb up). Rotate to palm‑down then palm‑up range you can control. Elbow at 90° by side.
Wrist Extensor Isometric (calm set) — 1×20–30s @ 3–4/10 (use only if flared)
  • Setup: Sit with forearm supported on a table, palm down, wrist just off the edge; elbow ~90° tucked by your side; shoulder relaxed.
  • Create the hold: With the other hand, apply gentle downward pressure on the back of your knuckles and match it so the wrist doesn’t move (an isometric). You should feel the top of the forearm working.
  • Hold & breathe: 20–30s steady breathing; rest 45–60s. One calm set is usually enough; do a second only if needed and still ≤3/10.
  • Angle options: Try wrist neutral or ~15–20° extension—use the angle that’s most comfortable.
  • Alternative: Stand at a wall and press the back of the hand into the wall with elbow at 90° for the same 20–30s hold.
  • Stop if sharp/nerve pain, tingling, or if pain >3/10 during or the next day.

Tuesday — Barefoot Walk–Jog + Foot/Calf Prep (~25–30 min)

Short‑Foot Drill — 3×20s/side
  • Gently lift your arch (ball of big toe toward heel) without curling toes. Hold; breathe.
Double‑Leg Calf Raise — 3×12 slow
  • Full range up/down 2–3s each; rise over big/second toe; no bouncing.
Tibialis Raise — 2×15
  • Back to wall; heels down; lift toes toward shins; slow tempo.
Run – Week 3 Session A — 5’ walk → 8× (60s jog / 60s walk) → 5’ walk volume bump
  • Cadence ~170–180; land under center; quiet feet; posture tall with slight forward lean from ankles.
  • If next‑morning calf/Achilles stiffness >60 min, repeat this volume before progressing.
Daily Shoulder Block (quick) — Scap Set • IR • Wall Angels • (optional) Push‑Up Plus
  • Keep the same cues from prior weeks. Do not push into pinchy ranges.

  • Hinge cleanly; straps allowed if elbow sensitive.
Chest‑Supported Row (neutral) — 3×10–12

Incline Push‑Up Plus — 2×10–12
  • Elbows 30–45°; add the plus at the top; choose incline that keeps pain ≤3/10.
Eccentric Wrist Extension — 3×8–10 (3s down)
  • Assist up with other hand; lower 3s; range is symptom‑guided.
Pronation/Supination (hammer) — 2×12 light
  • Hammer grip; rotate through comfortable range; elbow by side at 90°.
