Week 5 – Rest & Barefoot Training

Perform two barefoot sessions: start with a 10‑minute barefoot walk then incorporate 4–6 minutes of easy barefoot running (e.g., 4–6 × 1‑minute run/1‑minute walk). Add an extra 20–25‑minute barefoot walk on another day and continue foot‑strength work【254645292719844†L182-L187】.

Reminder: Keep warming up and cooling down even on rest days. Gentle stretching and mobility exercises will help your recovery.
