Week 8 – Rest & Barefoot Training

For your final week, aim for a 2‑mile barefoot run on soft surfaces and a 1‑mile run on smooth pavement or track. Keep focusing on quick cadence, mid‑foot strike and relaxed posture.

Reminder: Keep warming up and cooling down even on rest days. Gentle stretching and mobility exercises will help your recovery.
