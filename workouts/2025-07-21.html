Your Workout for Today: Monday, 21 July 2025

Here is your exact sequence for this morning's session. Aim for RPE 7-8 (feeling like you have 2-3 good reps left in the tank).

1. DYNAMIC WARM-UP (5 mins)

  • Arm Circles (forward & backward) - 30s each
  • Cat-Camel Stretch - 60s
  • Bodyweight Squats - 15 reps
  • Leg Swings (forward & side-to-side) - 30s each leg
  • Thoracic Rotations (on all fours) - 8 each side

2. MAIN WORK (35 mins)

Perform this as a superset. Do one set of A1, rest 30 seconds, do one set of A2, rest 60-90 seconds. Repeat for 4 total rounds.

A1: Goblet Squat 4 sets of 8 reps (with 20 kg kettlebell)
A2: Side Plank 4 sets of 30 seconds per side

After completing the above, perform the following for 3 sets, resting 60-90 seconds between sets.

B1: Romanian Deadlift (RDL) 3 sets of 10 reps (with dumbbells)

3. COOL-DOWN (5 mins)

  • Deep Squat Hold (prying hips open) - 60s total
  • Couch Stretch (for hip flexors) - 60s per side
  • Lying Hamstring Stretch - 60s per side

4. TONIGHT'S OPTIONAL MOBILITY

  • Focus: Shoulder band routine (see Evening Mobility Menu below for examples like Banded ER/IR).
