Week 4 – Rest & Barefoot Training

Keep 1–2 barefoot walks of 20 minutes. Begin to add very short 30–60‑second barefoot runs at the end of your walks (2–3 repeats) with a few minutes of walking between them. Pay attention to any discomfort—if your feet hurt, stick to walking.

Reminder: Keep warming up and cooling down even on rest days. Gentle stretching and mobility exercises will help your recovery.
