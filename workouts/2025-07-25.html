Your Workout for Friday, July 25

Total Time: 45 minutes | Focus: Full Body Strength C

Warm-up (5-7 minutes)

Cardio:

2 minutes of light cardio (Jumping Jacks, High Knees, or similar).

Mobility & Activation:

  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 10 reps
  • Thoracic Rotations: 8 reps per side
  • Arm Circles: 15 seconds forward & backward
  • Band Pull-Aparts: 2 sets of 15 reps
  • Wall Slides: 2 sets of 10 reps

Main Workout (30-35 minutes)

A1: Dumbbell Lunges

4 sets of 10-12 reps per leg. Rest 60 seconds.

Focus on keeping your torso upright.

B1: Dumbbell Rows

4 sets of 10-12 reps per arm. Rest 60 seconds.

Keep your back flat and pull the dumbbell towards your hip.

C1: Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs)

3 sets of 12-15 reps. Rest 60 seconds.

Focus on pushing your hips back. Use light weight to start.

D1: Plank

3 sets, hold for 30-45 seconds. Rest 45 seconds.

Cool-down (5 minutes)

Static Stretching:

  • Chest Stretch in Doorway: 30 seconds per side
  • Hamstring Stretch: 30 seconds per side
  • Quad Stretch: 30 seconds per side
  • Child's Pose: 60 seconds
