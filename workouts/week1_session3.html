Week 1 – Kettlebell & Core Circuit

Use your 20 kg kettlebell for a dynamic full‑body session to build power and core endurance.

Warm-Up (Karate Style)

This warm-up is inspired by Shotokan karate and is designed to prepare your body for a safe and effective workout. It includes dynamic and static stretching to improve flexibility, mobility, and blood flow.

  • Dynamic Warm-up (5-7 minutes):
    • Neck Rotations: Gently rotate your head in both directions.
    • Shoulder Rotations: Rotate your shoulders forwards and backwards.
    • Arm Swings: Swing your arms in large circles, both forwards and backwards.
    • Torso Twists: With your feet shoulder-width apart, twist your torso from side to side.
    • Hip Rotations: Rotate your hips in a circular motion.
    • Leg Swings: Swing your legs forwards, backwards, and side to side.
    • High Knees: Jog in place, bringing your knees up towards your chest.
    • Butt Kicks: Jog in place, kicking your heels up towards your glutes.
  • Static Stretching (3-5 minutes):
    • Hamstring Stretch: Sit on the floor with your legs extended. Reach for your toes, keeping your back straight. Hold for 20-30 seconds.
    • Quadriceps Stretch: Stand on one leg and pull your other foot towards your glute. Hold for 20-30 seconds.
    • Groin Stretch: Sit on the floor and bring the soles of your feet together. Gently press your knees towards the floor. Hold for 20-30 seconds.
    • Calf Stretch: Stand in a staggered stance and lean against a wall. Keep your back leg straight and your heel on the floor. Hold for 20-30 seconds.

Workout (≈35 minutes)

  • Kettlebell Swing: 3 sets of 15 reps. Drive the hips forward and allow the bell to swing to chest level.
  • Goblet Squat: 3 sets of 12 reps holding the kettlebell close to your chest.
  • One‑Arm Row: 3 sets of 12 reps per arm using the kettlebell or a dumbbell. Stabilise your torso with the opposite hand on a bench.
  • Glute Bridge: 3 sets of 15 reps. Squeeze glutes at the top; add the kettlebell across your hips for extra resistance.
  • Russian Twist: 3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side) holding the kettlebell or a weight plate.

Cooldown

Finish with about 5 minutes of gentle stretching (hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, and shoulders) to restore range of motion and reduce muscle soreness.
