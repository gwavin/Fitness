When to use the level-based branchHelp

Stay on Initial dose unless the next decision depends on a measured trough. Switch to Adjust after a previous level only when a previous level is actively driving the next action.

Pregnancy keeps specialist review visible, breastfeeding stays in the handoff summary, and myasthenia remains a hard stop.

Formulas and guardrailsReference

Adult body size calculations

IBW (Devine): 50 + 2.3 x (inches over 60) for males, 45.5 + 2.3 x (inches over 60) for females.

Adjusted dosing weight when actual body weight exceeds 120% of IBW: IBW + 0.4 x (ABW - IBW).

Cockcroft-Gault uses actual body weight if underweight, ideal body weight if non-obese, and adjusted dosing weight if obese.

Dosing bands in this prototype

Adult once-daily: CrCl >50 = 5 mg/kg (max 480 mg), CrCl 10 to 50 = 3 mg/kg (max 280 mg), CrCl <10 = 1.5 mg/kg STAT (max 160 mg), rounded to the nearest 40 mg.

Paediatric: 7 mg/kg once daily, capped at 560 mg.

Neonatal: 5 mg/kg with interval driven here by postnatal age.

Endocarditis synergy: 1 mg/kg with interval selected from renal function bands, but ongoing decisions should remain consult-led.
